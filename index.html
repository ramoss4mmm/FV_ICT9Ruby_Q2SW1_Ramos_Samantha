My 5 Favorite Disney Charater's



Character Information
Elsa

Elsa is one of the main characters in the movie "Frozen".

Olaf

Olaf is an animated character from the movie "Frozen" and is also created by Elsa.

Rapunzel

Rapunzel is a princess from "Tangeled" who was kidnapped by a crone that used her to stay young and healthy.

Belle

Belle is from the movie "Beauty and the Beast" who got imprisoned by a Beast to save her father's life, but then falls in love with the Beast.

Moana

Moana is from the movie "Moana" who has a deep love for the sea ever since she was a kid.
